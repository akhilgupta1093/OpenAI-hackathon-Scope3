The COVID -19 pandemic  has brought a preview of the 
disruption that climate change could bring — highlighting 
inequality and inadequacies in our global healthcare 
system, disrupting economies and political dynamics within and between countries, laying social and economic justice concerns, and changing the way we live, work, and do business. 
All businesses continue to face a challenging economic 
environment, due to market forces such as rising 
commodity prices, inflation, debt, and labor market gaps – and the policies and regulations that impact them
