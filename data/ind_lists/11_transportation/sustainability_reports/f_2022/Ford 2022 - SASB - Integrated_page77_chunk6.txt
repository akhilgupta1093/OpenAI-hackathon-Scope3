 T echnology for All
Making advanced technologies available to millions of people has been part of Ford’s DNA since its founder Henry Ford introduced the Model T to open highways to all mankind. That spirit guides our approach to new innovations, including remaining the only automaker to offer complimentary FordPass features such as remote vehicle starting and unlocking and SYNC 911 Assist that can automatically call emergency responders after a crash. Meantime, other automakers have moved these highly desirable features to subscriptions with monthly costs
