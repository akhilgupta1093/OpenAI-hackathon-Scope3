Consumer expectations and regulatory requirements  
for products to be made in an environmentally and 
socially responsible manner are increasing. Legislation is in development or has been recently passed requiring companies conduct actions to address broader human rights, environment, and responsible material due diligence in our business and supply chain. For example, the German Due Diligence Law coming into effect in January 2023, requires companies to address environmental and human rights in their business and through all levels of the supply chain to prevent or minimize risks
