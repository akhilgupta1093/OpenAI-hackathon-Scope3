Diversity, Equity & InclusionDiversity data (EEO -1) for U.S. employees: Tesla is a majority -minority company
We are proud to be a majority -minority company with a large representation of employees from communities 
that have long struggled to break through the historic roadblocks to equal opportunity in the U.S. As of 
December 31, 2020, 34% of our directors and vice presidents were non -white. This is a large percentage, 
considering that just 0.3% of our employees are director level and above at Tesla
