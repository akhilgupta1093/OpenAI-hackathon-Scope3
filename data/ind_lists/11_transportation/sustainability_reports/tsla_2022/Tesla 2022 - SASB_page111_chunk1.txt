We chose to utilize the Responsible Business Alliance’s (RBA) Validated Assessment Program (VAP) as the 
basis for our audits. The RBA’s VAP is an audit protocol globally recognized for its breadth of topics and 
stringent requirements for supplier completion. It covers nearly 200 checkpoints across labor, health and safety, 
environmental, ethical and management system topics. Audits are conducted by independent third parties that 
are approved by the RBA, and all auditors must undergo training by the RBA on the audit standard and its implementation ensuring a globally and industry -wide standardized approach
