Working HoursWorking hours are not to exceed the maximum set by local law. Further, a workweek should not be more than 60 hours per week, including overtime, except in emergency or 
uncommon circumstances. All overtime must be voluntary. Workers shall be allowed at least one day off every seven days, defin ed as a rest period of at least 24 consecutive 
hours every seven days. Suppliers must keep employee working hour and pay records in accordance with local and national laws andprovide records to Tesla upon request
