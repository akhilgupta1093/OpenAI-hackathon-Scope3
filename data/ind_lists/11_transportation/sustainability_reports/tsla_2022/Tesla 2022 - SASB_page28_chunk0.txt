29Committed to ensuring pay equity
T esla HR offers a Pay Equity & Pay Transparency educational course to everyone in the People Organization, 
with a focus on HR partners and recruiters. This course details what pay equity is, why it is important, how 
unconscious bias affects pay and hiring, best practices for hiring and compensation, and how each person can 
be an effective partner in helping the company achieve and maintain pay equity. A recorded version of the 
course is available to all hiring managers
