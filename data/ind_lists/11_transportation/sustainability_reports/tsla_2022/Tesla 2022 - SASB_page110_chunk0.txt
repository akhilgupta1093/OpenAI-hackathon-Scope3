
111Battery Supply Chain
OutlookOutlook for battery supply chain responsible sourcing: Formalization and expansion
Tesla’s battery responsible sourcing program is still relatively new. Yet, the program has achieved several 
important milestones this last year, including the development and initial implementation of a system to identify 
environmental and social risks in the battery supply chain and concrete progress towards mitigation and 
improving the situation of stakeholders impacted by Tesla’s battery supply chain
