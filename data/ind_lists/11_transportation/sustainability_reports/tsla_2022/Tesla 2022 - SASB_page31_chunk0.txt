Parental and family leave benefits
Whether it's family planning or support for employees spending time with their family after the birth or 
adoption of a child, Tesla provides benefit and leave options to all active full- time employees in the U.S.  
•Fertility Services including IVF, IUI and Egg/Embryo/Sperm Preservations up to $40,000 offered through 
Kindbody
•Adoption, up to $25,000 offered through Kindbody
•Third -Party reproduction services (donor & surrogacy), up to $25,000 offered through Kindbody
•16 weeks of Paid Family Leave
•Up to one week of paid time off as a new parent through T esla Child Bonding. This benefit can be taken 
following the birth or adoption of a child by an employee, their spouse or domestic partner
