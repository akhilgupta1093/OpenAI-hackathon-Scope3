Model 3 and Model Y score 5 -stars in all USNCAP categories 
After putting Model 3 and Model Y through a series of crash tests used as part of the New Car Assessment 
Program to calculate the likelihood of serious bodily injury for front, side and rollover crashes, the National 
Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) awarded each top safety ratings of 5 -stars in every category 
and subcategory
